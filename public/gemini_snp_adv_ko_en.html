When the market enters the greed zone, it is prudent to reduce exposure to overheated assets and increase allocation to defensive sectors or value stocks to prepare for potential corrections. Securing a higher cash position through profit-taking is also a sound strategy.

While a low VIX index might be interpreted as low market volatility, it also serves as a warning that investors could become complacent about risk. It is crucial to avoid excessive leveraged investments, adhere strictly to stop-loss principles, and prepare for unforeseen market shocks.
